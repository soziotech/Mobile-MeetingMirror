Travel

To reach the conference venue from the Munich airport, you can use the commuter train (S-Bahn) S8 or S1 to Karlsplatz (Stachus), for example. Karlsplatz (Stachus) is also the nearest subway station. The conference venue is within walking distance of the main central station (Hauptbahnhof).

The main campus of Technische Universität München for the workshops is about 20 minutes north by foot from the conference venue. Tramway #27 travels from Karlsplatz (Stachus) towards near the Technische Universität München campus.

Hotels

The conference venue is located in the center of Munich with several hotels nearby.

The venue and hotel information page for the conference provided by Munich Hotel Association lists some hotels in walking distance of the conference site and provides additional information.

You can also use the hotel information page of Munich’s official web site for orientation on accommodation in Munich. Please select a hotel of your preferences, and book the accommodiation directly at the hotel or via your preferred hotel booking service.
